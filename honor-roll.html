Nominate an Outstanding Individual

Welcome to the Honor Roll Nomination page for Wraca United. This is your chance to recognize the individuals who have made a lasting impact in grassroots football, not only within Moretele Section Northwest but also in neighboring settlements. Whether they are players, coaches, or community leaders, their contributions to the sport and the community deserve to be highlighted and celebrated.

The Honor Roll is a prestigious recognition that shines a light on the unsung heroes of our footballing community. We are still in the process of developing this special roll, but you can help us get started by nominating those who have gone above and beyond. Every nomination counts as we work to build this legacy together.

It’s important to act now and make sure your voice is heard—start nominating today, and let’s ensure these efforts don’t go unnoticed!
