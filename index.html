About Wraca

Wraca is more than just a football tournament. We offer comprehensive crash courses in football skills, fitness, and creative media production, including music and video creation. Our academy blends football culture with creativity, ensuring an all-rounded experience for our participants.

Video Production

Explore the world of sports media by producing high-quality football highlight reels, documentaries, and promotional videos.

The Wraca Cup is our flagship tournament, welcoming players of all skill levels. Compete, showcase your talent, and stand a chance to win exciting prizes.
